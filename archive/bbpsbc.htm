Barnet Blind & Partially Sighted Bowls Club

Barnet Blind and Partially Sighted Bowls Club welcomes new members.

An interest in sport and registered blind or partially sighted is all that is needed

The club is run by a small team of volunteers and players. Coaching, equipment and transport are available.

The club meets twice a week throughout the year on Tuesday and Friday afternoons and two hours fly by, enjoyed to the full by players and helpers. Come and have fun and find out what the game of bowls for the sight impaired is all about.
