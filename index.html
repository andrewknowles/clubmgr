Mill Hill Bowling Club

Welcome to your local bowling club in Mill Hill Park

About Us

Established in 1919 as a men only club we became mixed in 2001 when Mill Hill Ladies Club amalgamated with us. In 2003 HGH Bowls Club (Hendon Garden Hospital) joined us which further strengthened our numbers and today we have over 60 playing members. Friends and family are welcome. We have social in-club and arranged friendly matches with 35 other clubs, compete in the Prentice and President’s Cups and play against three clubs in Challenge Cup matches.

Club Activities

  • Short Mat Bowling

    We have recently purchased a new mat for indoor Short Mat Bowling

  • Whist

    During the winter why not come and join us for a game of Whist every Monday afternoon from 2pm

    Tea and cake at half time!

  • Matches and Competitions

    We have a full programme of friendly home and away matches and Club competitions so there is always something to look forward to.

    Use the Fixtures link above to see the fixtures list

  • Roll Ups

    We have roll-up games every day except Sunday beginning at 2.30pm. Names of those people wanting to play to be entered by 2.10pm.

    We break for tea after 9 ends. Games end around 5.00pm.

  • Coaching

    We offer free coaching to beginners and to any Club member who wants to improve their bowling or knowledge of the game.

    Use the Learn to Bowl link above for more details

  • Social Events

    We run events throughout the winter season for members and friends of the Club and on special occasions prepare meals for members.

  • Library

    We have books and DVDs which can be borrowed for use at home to introduce you to the game and some of its technicalities.

  • BB and PSBC

    Our Club members help the Barnet Blind and Partially Sighted Bowls Club members enjoy a game of bowls on a Tuesday. To find out more about BB&PSBC click on the relevant link.
