Learn to bowl with Mill Hill Bowling Club

Mill Hill Bowling Club has qualified coaches who will help you get started with the sport of Bowling.

Our coaches are also available for members to improve their game.

No equipment is necessary to start out. The club will lend you a set of bowls and some shoes whilst you are learning
